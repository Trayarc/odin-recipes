Protein Pancakes

What you need:

  • 100g of oats
  • 2 whole eggs
  • 20g of pumpkin puree
  • Your favorite protein powder
  • Blender
  • 15ml of vanilla essence

Steps:

  1. Put the oats into the blender
  2. Next put the eggs in
  3. Tip in the protein powder
  4. Pour in the vanilla essence
  5. Blend until its all mixed together
  6. Pour into a buttered pan
  7. Cook on a medium heat until bubbles form
  8. Finally, flip over and cook for roughly 2 minutes
  9. Enjoy
