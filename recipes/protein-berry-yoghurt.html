Protein and Berry Yoghurt

What you need:

  • 200g greek lite yoghurt
  • Protein powder
  • Mixed berrys work best, 150 gram

Method:

  1. Weigh off 200g of greek yoghurt into a bowl
  2. Add 1 serving of your favorite protein powder
  3. Mix it together
  4. Add 150g frozen berrys
  5. Mix again and viola! Yum!
